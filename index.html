Tapstr: The Legend Awakens

Tapstr is our very first indie game that is currently available on the Appstore. The gameplay involves defusing bombs that pop up on platforms while holding down on already defused bombs.

  • Download Tapstr on the Appstore

Tapstr2: Swole Uprising

Tapstr2 is the sequel to Tapstr. The story begins with BombSquad Sam taking to the skies in an attempt to defeat the endless waves of bombs that he failed to defuse in Tapstr. This game is currently awaiting approval by AppStore.

  • Download Tapstr2 on the Appstore

8-Bit SlapJack

8-Bit SlapJack brings back everyone's favorite card game. Now, players can challenge friends and foes to the ultimate battle of who has the fastest reaction time and secure all four Jacks to win the game!

  • Download 8-Bit SlapJack on the Appstore
